The Evergreen State

Washington, the "Evergreen State," is known for its forests, ski slopes, and tech scene. It's a land where nature meets technology.

Population: 7.958 million

Year the State was founded: 1889

Region within the USA: Pacific North-West

Average Income: $83,655

Explore Washington

Visit landmarks like the Space Needle, Olympic National Park, and Spokane Riverfront. Whether hiking, sightseeing, or skiing, there's something for everyone!
